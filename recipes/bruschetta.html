Bruschetta

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Tomatoes
  • Red onion
  • Garlic
  • Balsamic vinegar
  • Basil
  • Olive oil

Recipe

  1. Mix the onions, tomatoes, garlic, and basil. Add the balsamic vinegar and olive oil. Mix again and put away in the fridge for at least one hour.
  2. Cut the bread diagonally. Toast until light brown and serve with the mixture
