Bruschetta

Ingredients

  • Mascarpone cheese
  • Sugar
  • Espresso
  • Coffee liqueur
  • Ladyfinger biscuits
  • Cacao powder

Recipe

  1. Mix egg yolks with sugar, add mascarpone.
  2. Beat egg whites, fold into mascarpone mix.
  3. Combine coffee and liqueur, dip ladyfingers.
  4. Layer ladyfingers in dish, cover with mascarpone mix.
  5. Repeat layers, end with mascarpone on top.
  6. Refrigerate at least 4 hours.
  7. Dust with cacao powder before serving.
