Carbonara

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Pancetta
  • Olive oil
  • Egg yolks
  • Pecorino Romano cheese
  • Pepper & salt
  • Pasta water

Recipe

  1. Cook the pasta.
  2. Cook the pancetta in olive oil until crispy.
  3. Whisk egg yolks, pepper, salt, and cheese in a bowl.
  4. Drain the pasta and add to the pancetta. Add the cheese mixture and keep stirring to avoid scrambling the eggs. Add the pasta water. Season with pepper and serve immediatly with extra cheese.
